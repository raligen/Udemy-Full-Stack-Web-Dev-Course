Personal Song Selection

Top 3 Beatles Songs

Eight Days a Week

It was written by Paul McCartney and John Lennon based on McCartney's original idea. The song was recorded on 6 October 1964 during two sessions that together lasted nearly seven hours, with a fifteen-minute break in between.

Something

a song by the English rock band the Beatles from their 1969 album Abbey Road. It was written by George Harrison, the band's lead guitarist.

Here Comes the Sun

Harrison wrote "Here Comes the Sun" at the house of his friend Eric Clapton, in response to the dark mood surrounding the Beatles.
